WELCOME, OPERATOR

You found an alien console. The glyphs don't read like anything you've seen. But poke them, and they react differently.

That's the clue. Figure out what each glyph does, then push the energy core to 100%.

Three kinds of glyphs. Three kinds of behavior. Watch closely.

THREE GLYPH TYPES

VOLTAGE pumps energy in. The waveform jumps up.

INVERT flips the signal. The waveform mirrors itself.

LIMIT caps the output. The waveform hits a ceiling and stops.

THE LOOP

1. PROBE — Flip to PROBE mode, click a glyph, watch how it behaves. You get a few probes per round, so spend them well.

2. MARK — Back in MARK mode, tag each glyph with what you think it is. Your guess shows up under it, in both languages.

3. CALIBRATE — When all four are tagged, hit calibrate. Get them right and the core locks at 100%. Get one wrong and it overloads.

Every glyph you crack gets logged in your Lexicon for good. Decode all 12 and you own this console.

Shortcuts: P probe · M mark · I insight · C calibrate · R reset · L language
